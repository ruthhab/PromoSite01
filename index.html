THE STORY

Alex, a hopeless romantic and chronic overthinker, sends a risky text to their crush, Jordan. They stare at their phone, waiting—hoping—for a reply. A typing bubble appears… then vanishes. The message remains unread. Panic sets in.

Meanwhile, the world around Alex is unraveling. People everywhere are falling victim to the same fate—left on read, ghosted, ignored. The city descends into hysteria. Emergency sirens blare as officials issue public warnings:

"If you've been left on read for over 72 hours, report to the nearest safe zone."

In the streets, desperate texters collapse, their unread messages haunting them. A man mutters "wyd" over and over before passing out. Shadows flicker with eerie clown-faced 😂 emojis, lurking, taunting.

As society crumbles under the weight of unanswered texts, an underground resistance known as The Left On Read Order emerges. Their mission: to uncover the ultimate response—the one text so bold, so dangerous, that it forces a reply. Alex, now an unwilling hero in this madness, is led to their secret hideout. A hooded figure hands them a phone and whispers:

"There is only one way to break the cycle."

With trembling hands, Alex types the forbidden words:

"So ur just gonna ignore me?"

A chilling silence falls over the world. Time itself seems to pause. The typing bubble appears again.

Typing.
Typing.
TYPING.

Then… it disappears.

The screen fades to black. The horror continues.
